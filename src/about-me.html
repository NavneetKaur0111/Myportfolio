My name is Navneet Kaur. I was born in India. India is a country with many diverse cultures and people who believe in their traditions. They enjoy celebrating festivals with colors, lights and delicious food. They love to get together on special occasions and often do sleepovers at friend’s or relative’s houses.

I have a joint family in India which includes three nuclear families of my dad and his two brothers. Every night, I used to listen to stories from our grandparents, and in those stories, they try to give moral values to their children. I love having quality time with my family.

My hobbies are drawing cartoons and landscapes, coding, cooking, listening to music, and dancing. When I was a kid, I used to practice and improve my dance moves with my younger sister. My favorite style of dance are my culture’s traditional styles, Gidda and Bhangra. It needs so much energy and stamina to do those dances.

I came to Canada with a dream of becoming a skilled software developer. I want to master my profession and become one of the finest web developers around. Always doing my work with zeal. I want to put great effort into my work.

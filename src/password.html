What can happen if you got hacked?

Hacking can lead to serious problems. Hackers can damage your digital data, on purpose or by mistake. Unnecessary interference of hackers can lead to the loss of valuable data. Hackers can get access to your computer and can steal your sensitive information, your passwords, bank details that exists on your computer. Your information can be used for bad purposes.

Ways to make strong passwords-

Avoid using personal information as password
You should not use your personal information like your name or your pet name as a password. hacker can find that information from your social media accounts.
Avoid using same password for multiple logins
You should not use same password for different logins, as if hacker break through one of them, he will get acces to all those sites
Use random alphabets for passwords
You should use random alphabets for your passwords. Just because they are difiicult to guess, they can be helpful in securing your accounts.

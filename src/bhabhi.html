Bhabhi-game stategy

posted on Nov. 2, 2020 by Navneet

Bhabhi(opens in new tab) is an Indian card game. The goal of the game is to get rid of all the cards a player has. The last player who left with cards loses the game. The loser is called Bhabhi. Bhabhi can be played while spending quality time with your family and friends. It is a mind game that enhances critical thinking skills. Some strategies can be followed to win the game. The article below will give you some strategies to win the game.

Do the following calculations for your cards:
You should always count your number of safe turns before you start playing. Below are some calculations to count how many times, you can safely put down a higher card of a certain suit when somebody else is dealing:
Calculations Spade Club Heart Diamond
Assume you have following number of cards of each suit 4 1 5 3
Subtract your number of cards from 13 13 - 4 = 9 13 - 1 = 12 13 - 5 = 8 13 - 3 = 10
Divide the number left after subtraction by the total number of players (lets say you are 4 players playing) 9/4 = 2.2 12/4 = 3 8/4 = 2 10/4 = 2.5
Calculated Safe turns 2 3 2 2
Now, you have calculated the number of safe turns. Safe turn means that you can put a higher numbered card than the cards already in the current turn’s card deck. In this case, the number of safe turns are only 2 for spades, hearts, and diamonds and 3 rounds of clubs. There can be fewer safe turns on the corresponding suits than you calculated, but cannot be more than that.
Remember all the cards that are wasted:
It is important to remember the cards that were put down by all the players and are now in the waste cards pile. So, you can make guesses for other player’s cards and can make a game-changing move. Having a sharp memory can be the strongest point for a player.
Always try to get rid of your bigger cards first
If you are a dealer, you should try to remove all your bigger cards at the beginning of your game. Your smaller cards will help you avoid dealing and you can win easily.
Avoid dealing in the end:
When you are near to the end of the game, try to avoid dealing. If you get into the dealing position, put a small numbered card down. When somebody will not have a smaller numbered card, they will have to put a bigger numbered card. They will have to deal in the next card. That is what you can take advantage of.
Avoid increasing your cards:
There are some situations when you have to pick up the pile and play with it, so be careful and avoid those situations. Some of the situations when you have to pick up the pile are listed below:
  • When somebody is unable to follow the suit and your card is the biggest numbered card in the current turn pile, then you have to pick up the pile. So if you have a smaller card in that situation, put that down.
  • When it is not your turn, but you put a card down by mistake.
  • When you have a card to follow the suit, but you put a card down from another suit, by chance.

Now, you know some strategies to win the game. Try implementing them and see the results.
